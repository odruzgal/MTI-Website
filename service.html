Every semester members have the opportunity to work on a different project team. Read below to see the teams for our upcoming semester!

Machine Learning Arbitrage

This team will be focusing on understanding the foundations of machine learning by implementing a data structure that trains on historical correlational relationships to make predictions on future options.

Discretionary Trading Data Collection

This team will be implementing a system to read through public domain webpages and create a predictive index for a small group of related stocks in order to gather generalized data at a highly efficient rate.
