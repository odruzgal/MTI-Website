About Us

Founded in 2024, Michigan Quantitative Computing is an innovative project-based mathematical computing club at the University of Michigan

Our Purpose

MQC was formed to be an outlet for students with a passion for mathematics and computer science to practice their skills in real-world contexts.

Members have the opportunity to form and join a different project team every semester. Covering topics from statistical arbitrage to protein synthesis to neural networks and public database word processing, these teams can explore infinite possible methods to processing and understanding scientific and economic data. As long as your team can write the algorithm, there is no idea too crazy to test!

In order to encourage members to take risks and explore uncharted topics, we backtest all financial algorithms over years of data, as opposed to holding an active fund. This allows us to uphold a comfortable, stress-free environment within our community.
